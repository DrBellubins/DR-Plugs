Delay/Reverb plugin

Delay rate has 4 modes (millisecond, normal sync, triplet sync, dotted sync).

Has 3 knobs (Diffusion Amount, Diffusion Size, Diffusion Quality).

Octave echos (both delay and reverb), can be set to -48 to +48, can be randomly pitched to different octaves, or start from lowest and to highest octave.
Pitched echos can have the option to be randomly pitched between different octaves, or pitch progressively upward/downward by given cents.

Ping-pong with left-right and mid-side modes (ping-pong between mono and stereo set by spread parameter).

Pre-post toggles for spread (haas filter), low pass/high pass, and distortion.

Seperate menu with toggles for different deelay modes broken into catagories. Playback direction, pitch, chaos.

Payback direction:
(Toggle) Forward - Play echos normally
(Toggle) reverse - Play echos in reverse
(Toggle) Ping-pong - Ping-pong echos between playing normally and reverse

Pitch:
(Toggle) Enable - Enables any and all pitch fx, is next to catagory label.

--- Types ---
(Toggle) Progressive - Progressively pitch echos up or down (depending on pitch range set, if -48 to +48, start at -48 if up toggled, +48 if down toggled).
(Toggle) Random - Randomly pitches echos up/down/both depending on range, and rnd mode.

(Toggle) Ping-Pong - If progressive, steps upward/downward back and forth. If not progressive, then lowerValue & upperValue back and forth.

--- Progressive ---
(Switch) Up/Down - If progressive, this is the direction of pitch stepping, otherwise it decides whether pitch is set to lowerValue, or upperValue.

--- Random ---
(3-way toggle) Random mode - whether to pitch randomly upwards, downwards, or both.

Chaos:
(Toggle) Enable - Enables any and all chaos fx, is next to catagory label.

Other:
(Toggle) Enable - Enables any and all other fx, is next to catagory label.
(Toggle) M/S Swell - Swell from mono to stereo.
